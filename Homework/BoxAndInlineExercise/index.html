The Cottage Garden

Thecottage gardenis a distinct style of garden that uses an informal design, dense paintings, and a mixture of oranamental and edible plants.

The Cottage Garden originated inEnglandand its history can be traced back for centuries although they were re-invented in the 1870's England, when stylized versions were formed as a reaction to the more structured and rigorously maintained English estate gardens.

The earliest cottage gardens were more practical than their modern descendants, with an emphasis on vegetables and herbs, along with some fruit trees.
